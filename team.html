TEAM

Sparsh Sharma

Sparsh Sharma is an actor, writer, producer and the founder of Flying Horse Motion Pictures. He left N.D.A and engineering to fulfill his aspirations as an actor. He did theater under the direction of eminent theater personalities, Shri Satish Anand and Dr. Jaidev Taneja. After doing theatre for 8 years and performing all over India , he landed in the city of dreams, Mumbai. While doing various versatile roles for television in supernatural and reality shows, Sparsh Sharma was spotted by renowned Director Mr. Kabir Sadanand who was quite impressed with his acting skills after watching his audition tape and gave him his first break on the big screen with a prominent role in the movie “Gollu and Pappu” produced by “Viacom 18”. Mr. Kabir Sadanand was highly impressed with Sparsh's acting and production skills and gave him a prominent role in his next successful venture, “Fugly” produced by Akshay Kumar and Ashwini Yardi, under their banner, 'Grazing Goats', starring Jimmy Shergill and Olympian boxer Vijender Singh. Sparsh Sharma played the crazy character "Koki" which was loved so much by the audience and appreciated a lot by the film fraternity. That paved the way to stardom for Sparsh Sharma and was a turning point in Sparsh's career following which he acted in various movies as the lead actor.
Many of his movies are slated to release soon and are in various stages of production. He has also recently written, directed and produced the super hit short psychological thriller “CHOOL”. His production house FLYING HORSE MOTION PICTURES and his world class team aim at making quality cinema content for the Indian and international audience.

Upinder Randhawa

Upinder Randhawa is a film maker and media figure who commenced his career working for a number of Asian Community Radio Stations. His media career came into further prominence when working as a Presenter for UK lifestyle channel Sangat TV. He was seen to be covering live footage of the 2011 England Riots within the West Midlands. Upinder's participation was commended by PM David Cameron in the House of Commons for its commitment to social responsibility. He was also featured in 10 Heroes of the London Riots in New York’s Time Magazine. Upinder was voted in at number 41 for DesiClub.com’s Top 50 Coolest Desis of 2011. Upinder made his acting debut in the Punjabi film Mirza: The Untold Story, playing a character role, before propelling himself further and becoming a film producer. A subsequent release in which he also had a character role was Desi Munde in 2016. Kirdaar, his first film production, is what he refers to as his film school, through which he learnt and applied the necessary expertise to complete an astonishing production. This film has featured in several international film festivals and is due imminent release. As well as this, Upinder carries out compelling interviews for Punjab2000 with prominent figures within the Asian media industry. In 2016, Upinder spearheaded a project set-up for a Channel in Canada before returning to the U.K. to produce and present shows for a Sky lifestyle channel. Alongside his television achievements, Upinder is set to be working on two main projects this year: one as a producer, the second as an actor for a Bollywood production. Upinder will be demonstrating his extensive industry knowledge as a judge on the Asian Beauty Pageant..
