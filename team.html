TEAM

Sparsh Sharma

Sparsh Sharma is an Actor, Writer, Producer and the founder of FLYING HORSE MOTION PICTURES. He left lucrative careers like N.D.A and Engineering to fulfill his aspirations as an actor. He did theater under the direction of eminent theater personalities Sh. Satish Anand ji and Dr. Jaidev Taneja ji. After doing theatre for 8 years and performing all over India , he landed in the city of dreams - Mumbai.
Mr. Kabir Sadanand spotted Sparsh's talent after seeing his audition tape and gave him a prominent role in "GOLLU AUR PAPPU" , Produced by "Viacom 18". Mr. Kabir Sadanand was so much impressed with Sparsh's acting skills as well as production skills that he gave him a prominent role in his next venture " FUGLY ", produced by "Grazing Goats". "FUGLY " was the turning point in Sparsh's career and afterwards he acted in various movies as the lead actor. All the movies are slated to release in 2017. He has written , directed and produced CHOOL series which is a psychological thriller. During the making of CHOOL , he realized his potential as a writer, director and producer apart from his acting skills and founded the production house FLYING HORSE MOTION PICTURES..
