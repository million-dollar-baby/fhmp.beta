TEAM

Sparsh Sharma

Sparsh Sharma is an Actor , Writer , Producer and the founder of FLYING HORSE MOTION PICTURES. He is a college dropout from Delhi University who aspired to become an actor and he left lucrative careers like N.D.A , Engineering etc to fulfill his dreams. He did theater under the direction of eminent theater personalities "Mr. Satish Anand " and " Mr. Jaidev Taneja ". After doing theatre for 8 years and performing all over India , he landed in the city of dreams Mumbai.
Mr. Kabir Sadanand spotted Sparsh's talent after seeing his audition tape and gave him a prominent role in " GOLLU AUR PAPPU " , Produced by "Viacom 18 " . Mr. Kabir Sadanand was so much impressed with Sparsh's acting skills as well as production skills that he gave him a prominent role in his next venture " FUGLY " , produced by " Grazing Goats ". "FUGLY " was the turning point in Sparsh's career and he has acted in various movies as lead , one of the leads afterwards. All the movies are slated to release in 2017. Currently Sparsh Sharma is busy in the dubbing of one of his movies as lead. He has written , directed and produced CHOOL series which is a psychological thriller . During the making of CHOOL ,he founded the production house FLYING HORSE MOTION PICTURES as he realized his potential as a writer , director and producer apart from his acting skills.
