TEAM

Sparsh Sharma

Sparsh Sharma is an actor, writer, producer and the founder of Flying Horse Motion Pictures. He left N.D.A and engineering to fulfill his aspirations as an actor. He did theater under the direction of eminent theater personalities, Shri Satish Anand and Dr. Jaidev Taneja. After doing theatre for 8 years and performing all over India , he landed in the city of dreams, Mumbai. While doing various versatile roles for television in supernatural and reality shows, Sparsh Sharma was spotted by renowned Director Mr. Kabir Sadanand who was quite impressed with his acting skills after watching his audition tape and gave him his first break on the big screen with a prominent role in the movie “Gollu and Pappu” produced by “Viacom 18”. Mr. Kabir Sadanand was highly impressed with Sparsh's acting and production skills and gave him a prominent role in his next successful venture, “Fugly” produced by Akshay Kumar and Ashwini Yardi, under their banner, 'Grazing Goats', starring Jimmy Shergill and Olympian boxer Vijender Singh. Sparsh Sharma played the crazy character "Koki" which was loved so much by the audience and appreciated a lot by the film fraternity. That paved the way to stardom for Sparsh Sharma and was a turning point in Sparsh's career following which he acted in various movies as the lead actor.
Many of his movies are slated to release soon and are in various stages of production. He has also recently written, directed and produced the super hit short psychological thriller “CHOOL”. His production house FLYING HORSE MOTION PICTURES and his world class team aim at making quality cinema content for the Indian and international audience.

Harmeet Chowdhary

Harmeet Chowdhary is the Chief Business Strategist and Creative producer of Flying Horse Motion Pictures. He is also a renowned Film Consultant, International Motivational Speaker and a Talent Promoter in the Film Industry. He has a background from The United Kingdom, London and has spent several years studying and enhancing his entrepreneurial Skills in various Creative sectors in the UK. He has been up-skilled by Mr. John Twitchen (Creative Director BBC) on Advanced Communication Skills and Art of Film Production. His Film Consulting skills have made him the forerunner for promoting and successfully managing Bollywood Movie Campaigns and is a Success Coach for various artists in the Industry. He was recently awarded with the Shaheed Bhagat Singh award for the recognition and contribution towards the Media and Education Industry. He also has as a diversified international portfolio consisting of his core Marketing and production Teams based in the UK and Thailand which work in coordination with the in-house Mumbai and Delhi production Teams. He has been instrumental in raising finance for various Movie Projects and has consistently advised the Team on business aspects of Film Production.
