Flying Horse Motion Pictures is an Indian Production House founded by Sparsh Sharma. Our Vision is to make contemporary, commercial and critically acclaimed cinema . We believe in making a mark in Indian Film Industry as well as internationally by our productions. After the success of "Chool" ( short feature film series ) and gaining positive reviews from audience as well as from the industry , Sparsh Sharma decided to make a feature film. We are all set to make our first " Hindi " feature film and It's genre is " drama ".

Flying Horse Motion Pictures is determined to support every possible talent . The biggest strength of Flying Horse Motion Pictures is its core values and core team which brings vast experiences and creativity to the production house.
